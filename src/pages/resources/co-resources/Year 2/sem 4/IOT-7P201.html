Subject Description

Over the past few years, IoT has become one of the most important technologies of the 21st century. IoT applications use machine learning algorithms to analyze massive amounts of connected sensor data in the cloud. Today it is possible to build different IoT solutions such as shopping system, infrastructure management in both urban and rural areas.
