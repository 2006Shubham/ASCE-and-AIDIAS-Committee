Subject Description

This course provides a comprehensive study of Cloud concepts and capabilities across the various Cloud service models including Infrastructure as a Service (IaaS), Platform as a Service (PaaS),Software as a Service (SaaS). It consists of topics like cloud service models, virtualization and cloud infrastructure, and security and management of cloud.
